Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women(IGDTUW).

With its inception in 2015, every year, the two-day extravaganza has witnessed a phenomenol gathering of students from all domains.

The event is back for its SIXTH edition where it aims to be a platform to test engineering skills and showcase prowess.
